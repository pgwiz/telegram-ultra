Admin Commands
CommandDescription
/upcook [cookies]Update the cookies.txt used for age-gated or member-only content. Paste cookie text as argument, or send a cookies.txt file.
/allow <seconds> Open a time-limited OTP-free login window (max 300 seconds). During the window the Quick Login button appears on the login page — anyone who knows their Chat ID can log in without receiving an OTP code.

  • Two-Factor: OTP code sent to your Telegram account
  • Quick Login: Use /allow botp in Telegram for a direct login link (no OTP)
  • Features: View downloads, schedule tasks, manage files, and more
How to Find Your Chat ID

Your Chat ID is required to log in to this dashboard.

  1. Open Telegram and find the Hermes bot.
  2. Send /start or /chatid.
  3. The bot replies with your Chat ID in a copyable monospace block.
  4. Paste that number on the login page to request your OTP.

Audio & Video Quality
FormatDetails
Audio (default) Best audio stream → MP3. Format: bestaudio[ext=m4a]/bestaudio/best, VBR quality 0 (highest).
Video (standard) Up to 1080p merged with best audio → MP4. Automatic fallback if merge is unavailable.
Video (high / best) No resolution cap — downloads 4K, 8K, or whatever is highest. Use /dv high or /downloadv2.
Audio (high / best) Highest bitrate audio available, VBR quality 0. Use /da high or /downloadv2 mp3.
Playlists Batch download up to all tracks. /playlist lets you pick audio or video. /playlistv2 auto-selects video.
Search results Tap a search result → choose Audio (MP3) or Video (MP4) before download starts.

Common Issues
ProblemSolution
"Requested format is not available" The video restricts certain quality levels. The bot automatically falls back to the best available format. If it still fails, try /dv and select a lower quality.
Age-gated content fails Ask the admin to update cookies via /upcook with a valid YouTube session cookie.
Playlist downloads stop partway Some playlist items may be unavailable. The bot uses --ignore-errors to skip bad items and continue.
Login OTP not received Check that you started the bot first (/start). Use /allow botp for a direct login link instead.
Session expired Sessions last 10 minutes by default. Log in again. The admin can extend SESSION_TTL_SECS in the server configuration.
